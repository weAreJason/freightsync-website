Built From
the Cab Up.

FreightSync exists because one owner-operator got tired of accepting loads based on gross rate — and watching the profit disappear by the time he got home.

Our Story

The Problem We Lived

As an owner-operator, you've been there. A broker posts a load for $1,400. Sounds decent. You accept it. Then you fill up — $180 in diesel. You deadhead 40 miles to the pickup. Tolls add another $22. By the time you're home, you've netted less than $1,100 on a run you thought was profitable.

The industry has always shown you gross rate. Nobody showed you the truth. That's why FreightSync was built.

We're an Oklahoma City–based company founded by a former owner-operator who left the cab and started writing code nights and weekends to solve a problem he couldn't find a solution to anywhere else. FreightSync is the tool he wished existed when he was still running loads.

We're part of a larger vision — a portfolio of companies being built to serve the people who keep America moving. FreightSync is the first, and it's the one closest to our hearts.
